About US

EQMS At a Galance

EQMS Consulting Limited (EQMS) is the largest sustainability multidisciplinary consulting firm in Bangladesh . Headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh and International office at Toronto, Canada, EQMS has satellite offices in other five districts of Bangladesh. EQMS is the leading-reputable and most experienced Private Sector Environmental & Social Consultancy Company in Bangladesh provides expertise services for transfer of appropriate, affordable, acceptable and no environmental damage pertaining technology to the requirement of small, medium and large industries with particular emphasis on Energy and Power, Road/ Infrastructure, Agriculture and Food processing, Chemical and Fertilizer, Oil and Gas Exploration Sector, Cement and Paper, Leather and Textile, Garment, Pharmaceutical, Wastes and by products utilization, Water Resource Management, Sanitation and Pollution Control Management Plan, etc. EQMS is ISO 9001:2015, 14001:2015 and OHSAS 18001 certified company, is the leading provider for environmental and social impact assessment, social consulting, biodiversity conservation, engineering and industrial solution and sustainability related services. EQMS has its own environmental and analytical laboratory, which is internationally recognized and certified. EQMS is a Department of Inspection for Factories & Establishments (DIFE), Government of Bangladesh (GoB) approved organization compliance to the requirements of Bangladesh Labour Rule 2015, authorized for testing & certification of Lifting Equipment and Pressure Vessels and initial & Detail Engineering Assessments (DEA) of Structural, Electrical Safety and Building Fire Safety for industrial Buildings
