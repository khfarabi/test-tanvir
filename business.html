Site Selection/ Scoping Study


EQMS assists sponsors/ clients in selecting an environmentally and socially friendly site for setting up the concerned project with due consideration of natural/ existing environmental conditions and sufficiently available resources as well as in terms of social issues of the proposed project. EQMS will assess the regulatory requirements for the Project and reconnaissance survey of the Project site and surrounding area. Also delineated the scope of ESIA study.

Environmental and Social Due Diligence


EQMS is a pioneer to conduct Environmental and Social Due Diligence (ESDD) for financial institution's and its outcome should be factored into the decision-making process for proceeding with a transaction. EQMS’ expert can involve a simple desktop review or require a site visit with the use of technical experts, if necessary, to understand potential environmental and social risks associated with business activities and review a client’s/investee’s compliance with the financial institution’s environmental and social requirements. EQMS’ experts are well aware of IFC PS, EP, ADB SPS guidelines for conducting this ESDD.

Environmental and Social Audit during Construction and Operation Phase


EQMS’s experts are fully capable of doing the EHS and Social Compliance Audit during construction and operational phases to Assess the Project's compliance with environment, health, safety and social requirements as per IFC PS, EP, ADB & ILO along with the national policy and rules and Good International Industry Practices (GIIP) including elements of ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001 Occupational Health and Safety Management System. EQMS’s expert will also Review the company's existing management system, standard operating procedures (SOPs) and training in relation to EHS&S and identification of areas for improvement/ enhancement.

Geographical Information Systems (GIS) and Remote Sensing (RS)


EQMS has a separate division for Environmental & Social mapping has strong proficiency and broad experience in the application of GIS and RS technology in various sectors. This Division provides services in satellite image processing, geo-referencing and map preparation, land use and land cover classification, flood coverage and risk analysis, disaster monitoring and damage assessment including erosion mapping. Also provide analysis of spatial and temporal data derived from satellite images using ArcGIS and ERDAS IMAGINE software.

Social Impact Assessment


Social impact assessment includes the processes of analyzing, monitoring and managing the intended and unintended social consequences, both positive and negative, of planned interventions (policies, programs, plans, projects) and any social change processes invoked by those interventions. EQMS has vast experiences on SIA studies for different development projects of public and private sectors to comply the national and international guidelines. EQMS has multidisciplinary team to carry out the social impact assessment study.

Safeguard and Social Action Planning


EQMS has experiences to carry out the following safeguard and social action planning studies Identification of project impacts and affected populations; Land Acquisition Plan (LAP); Resettlement Action Plan/Resettlement Plan; Income and Livelihood Restoration Plan (ILRP) for Affected Populations (PAPs); Gender Action Plan (GAD); Indigenous People’s Plan (IPP); Prepare project-specific legal/policy framework for land acquisition and compensation; Prepare Resettlement Assistance Payment Mechanism; Prepare a Detailed Resettlement Budget; Prepare time-bound implementation schedule of RAP/RP;

Environmental Quality Monitoring


EQMS maintains widely experienced and capable professionals to conduct environmental quality parameters monitoring. The parameters such as microclimatic conditions, ambient air quality, flue gas analysis, noise levels, surface and groundwater quality, soil/sediment quality and aquatic organism (Phytoplankton, Zooplankton & Benthos) examined as part of comprehensive monitoring program/ plan in EQMS’ own accredited laboratory (ISO 170020 & ISO 170025). A team of experienced field investigators, site engineers, and chemists under the supervision of project managers undertake extensive environmental monitoring studies/ exercises through the application of standard sampling techniques/ plan.
